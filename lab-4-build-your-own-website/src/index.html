About Me

Hi, I'm James P — a self-taught programmer with a passion for clean code, problem-solving, and turning creative ideas into working products.

I specialize in C# with Unity, and applications using C++ or Java with a strong interest in building web applications and video games. My work often emphasizes functionality, clarity, and maintainability — I believe code should not only work, but work well.

As someone who learned to code independently, I'm constantly exploring new tools, frameworks, and development practices. I enjoy bridging the gap between logic and creativity, whether I'm crafting a UI or scripting game mechanics.

Much of my creative inspiration comes from horror fiction, both in films and literature. That influence often shows in the themes, mood, or mechanics of the games and projects I create.

My strongest inspirations are novels like House of Leaves, and films like Alien and Event Horrizon, my favourite horror media being John Carpanter's The Thing.

Outside of coding, I enjoy engaging with art, music, and storytelling, which help shape how I think about user experience and interactivity.

If you're interested in collaborating, have a project idea, or just want to chat about code, games, or horror — feel free to reach out!
